About Me

Naufal Adyatma

Flutter developer || Game developer

Meet Naufal Adyatma, a young and ambitious developer with a passion for creating innovative and engaging mobile and desktop applications. With experience in both Flutter and Android Studio, Naufal has honed his skills in creating visually stunning and intuitive apps. In addition to mobile app development, Naufal has also delved into the world of game development, utilizing Construct 3 to create engaging and interactive games for web and mobile platforms.

At just 18 years old, Naufal has already made a name for himself in the tech community, having learned to code at the young age of 16. His dedication and hard work have propelled him to become a skilled developer in a short amount of time.

In his portfolio, you will find a wide range of applications and games that showcase his diverse range of skills and talents. From desktop apps that are both functional and visually appealing, to mobile games that offer endless entertainment and engagement, Naufal's portfolio is a testament to his passion for creating high-quality products.

Naufal's unique blend of technical skills and creative vision make him a valuable asset to any team. He is always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer, and is excited to take on new projects and push the boundaries of what's possible.

Japanese Midnight Car

"Japanese Midnight Car" is an Android app that features a library of Japanese cars commonly used for road racing. Users can browse, schedule and track late night rides, set reminders, and record their rides. | It's perfect for car enthusiasts and racing enthusiasts who are interested in Japanese late model cars.

Desktop App

A cross-platform desktop application

SignToMe

"SignToMe" is a Windows app that sends reminders for Adzan (Islamic call to prayer). It allows users to schedule Adzan sessions and set reminders for regular practice. It also includes a feature for recording and tracking progress over time. It's perfect for Muslims who want to stay on top of their religious practices.
